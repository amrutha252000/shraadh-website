Pind Daan at Badrinath

2 Nights / 3 Days Itinerary, Ex Haridwar

Rs. 14,500/- per person

Trip Summary

Day 1: Haridwar to Badrinath (Approx. 320 km / 10-12 hrs). Overnight stay in Badrinath.

Day 2: Pind Daan Rituals and Sightseeing in Badrinath. Optional visit to Mana Village. Overnight stay in Badrinath.

Day 3: Badrinath to Haridwar (Approx. 320 km / 10-12 hrs). Departure.

Inclusions

  • Private vehicle for entire journey (Haridwar to Badrinath).
  • Accommodation for 2 nights in Badrinath.
  • Daily breakfast and dinner.
  • Assistance for arranging Pind Daan rituals at Brahma Kapal with local priest.

Exclusions

  • Dakshina for Pandit (to be paid directly).
  • Any additional sightseeing or excursions not mentioned.
  • Personal expenses, tips, or special pujas at Badrinath Temple.

Detailed Itinerary

Day 1

Haridwar to Badrinath (Approx. 320 km / 10-12 hrs)

Begin the journey from Haridwar, passing through Rishikesh and gradually entering the scenic mountain roads. Enroute, you will cross the holy Panch Prayag (five sacred confluences of rivers). Drive continues via Joshimath. On arrival in Badrinath, check into the hotel and relax. In the evening, you may visit the Badrinath Temple for a quick darshan.

Overnight Stay: Badrinath (Hotel Mangalam Badrinath)

Day 2

Pind Daan Rituals and Sightseeing

Begin the day with a holy dip in the Tapt Kund. Proceed towards Brahma Kapal on the banks of the Alaknanda river for the Pind Daan and Shraddh rituals, guided by local priests. After completing the rituals, you have the option to visit Mana Village, the last Indian village near the Indo-Tibetan border, known for its mythological importance.

Overnight Stay: Badrinath

Day 3

Badrinath to Haridwar (Approx. 320 km / 10-12 hrs)

After breakfast, check out and begin the return journey. On the way back, you can stop briefly at the Prayags or other spiritual spots you might have missed. Arrive in Haridwar by evening and get dropped at the railway station or hotel for your onward journey.
