A Step-by-Step Guide to Pind Daan with Us

By Shraadh Team | July 30, 2025
Back to All Posts

When you choose to honour your ancestors, you deserve a journey that feels sacred and yours. Whether your family prefers a serene riverside ritual, a spiritual trek, or a visit to an ancient architectural wonder, we help create an experience that is as meaningful as the rite itself.

Our process begins with you, your family’s tradition, comfort, and vision for the journey.

What You Can Expect:

  • Tailored to Your Belief & Interest: Whether you want to perform Shraadh at the confluence of Prayagraj, amidst the architectural depth of Gaya, or beneath the Akshayavat at Varanasi, we guide you to the right location.
  • Curated Experience Type:
    • Spiritual Sojourn: including sacred rivers, temples, and meditative spaces
    • Architectural Pilgrimage: encompassing Kashi's ghats, Badrinath’s temple carvings
    • Adventure + Ritual: which entails forest walks in Gokarna, coastal serenity in Rameshwaram

    (You can even combine two or all three, it’s entirely up to your family’s rhythm.)

  • Personalised Food & Stay: From sattvik thalis to region-specific offerings, and from guesthouses to homestays, we take care of it all.
  • Multilingual Priests and Full Ritual Support: Whether you’re Tamil, Telugu, Bengali, Marwari, or Maharashtrian, we assign knowledgeable priests aligned with your customs.

A Ritual Beyond the Checklist

This isn’t just a ritual. It’s a story you’re completing, a prayer you’re offering, and a journey you’ll remember. We walk with you from the moment you decide to do the ritual until the last offering is made.
