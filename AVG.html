Detailed Itinerary

Day 1

Arrival in Ayodhya - Ram Janmabhoomi Darshan

Arrival at Ayodhya by train or flight. Meet & transfer to hotel. Check-in and freshen up. Visit the sacred sites.

  • Shri Ram Janmabhoomi Temple
  • Hanuman Garhi
  • Kanak Bhawan
  • Saryu Ghat for evening aarti.

Overnight Stay: Ayodhya (Hotel Raghuvar Inn)

Day 2

Ayodhya to Varanasi - Evening Ganga Aarti

After breakfast, check out and proceed to Varanasi (approx. 5-6 hours by road). On arrival, check-in at hotel near temple/ghat area.

  • Dashashwamedh Ghat Ganga Aarti: A spectacular fire ritual.
  • Optional Boat Ride: Witness the sacred ghats from the river at sunset.

Overnight Stay: Varanasi (HHW Hotel Varanasi)

Day 3

Varanasi Temples & Ancestral Tarpan

Morning temple visits and rituals.

  • Kashi Vishwanath Jyotirlinga
  • Annapurna Devi & other major temples.
  • Tarpan Ritual: Holy dip and offerings for ancestors at the Ganga ghats.

Overnight Stay: Varanasi

Day 4

Varanasi to Gaya - Vishnupad Temple Darshan

Check out after breakfast and proceed to Gaya (approx. 6 hrs drive). Arrival & check-in at hotel.

  • Vishnupad Temple: Visit the central site for Pind Daan rituals.
  • Evening Ritual Planning: Light a lamp and meet the appointed purohit.

Overnight Stay: Gaya (Hotel Taj Darbar)

Day 5

Gaya - Main Pind Daan Ceremony & Ritual Sites

The main day for performing the sacred Pind Daan ceremony.

  • Morning Rituals: Holy bath in the Falgu River, perform Sankalp, offer Pinds, and conduct Tarpan.
  • Akshay Vat Tree: Make offerings under the eternal banyan tree.
  • Optional Pretshila Hill Visit.

Overnight Stay: Gaya

Day 6

Bodh Gaya Visit (Optional) & Departure

After breakfast, you have the option to visit Bodh Gaya (13 km away) before your departure.

  • Mahabodhi Temple: UNESCO World Heritage Site.
  • Bodhi Tree & Monasteries.

After your visit, transfer to Gaya Airport or Railway Station for your departure.
