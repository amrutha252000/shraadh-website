Detailed Itinerary

Day 1

Arrival in Gaya - Darshan at Vishnupad Temple

Arrive at Gaya by train or flight. Transfer to your hotel near the Vishnupad Temple. In the evening, visit the temple to offer prayers, light a lamp, and meet your appointed priest to plan the next day's ceremony.

Overnight Stay: Gaya (Hotel Taj Darbar)

Day 2

Main Ritual Day - Pind Daan Ceremony

Begin early with a ritual bath in the Falgu River. Under the guidance of your priest, perform the Sankalp, offer Pindas, and conduct Tarpan. Complete the ritual by feeding Brahmins and cows. Visit the sacred Akshay Vat tree.

Overnight Stay: Gaya

Day 3

Optional Bodh Gaya Visit - Departure

After breakfast, you have the option to visit Bodh Gaya to see the Mahabodhi Temple and Bodhi Tree. Later, check out from the hotel and transfer to Gaya Airport or Railway Station for your return journey.
