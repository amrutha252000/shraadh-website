Detailed Itinerary

Day 1

Arrival in Kashi - Ganga Snan & Evening Aarti

Arrive at Varanasi Airport/Railway Station and transfer to your hotel. In the afternoon, proceed to the ghats for Tarpan rituals and a holy dip in the Ganga. Witness the grand evening Ganga Aarti at Dashashwamedh Ghat.

Overnight Stay: Varanasi (Broadway Hotel Varanasi)

Day 2

Varanasi Temple Visits & Local Sightseeing

Begin the day with a visit to the sacred Kashi Vishwanath Jyotirlinga. Explore other important temples like Annapurna Devi, Sankat Mochan, Durga Kund, and Tulsi Manas Mandir.

Overnight Stay: Varanasi

Day 3

Journey from Kashi to Gaya - Vishnupad Temple

After breakfast, check out and drive to Gaya (approx. 6 hours). After checking into your hotel, visit the Vishnupad Temple in the evening to light a lamp for your ancestors and prepare for the next day's rituals.

Overnight Stay: Gaya (Hotel Taj Darbar)

Day 4

Gaya - Pind Daan Ceremony

The main day for performing the sacred Pind Daan ceremony. The rituals include a holy bath in the Falgu River, performing Sankalp, offering Pinds, and conducting Tarpan with a priest's guidance. Also, visit the Akshay Vat Tree for timeless offerings.

Overnight Stay: Gaya

Day 5

Optional Visit to Bodh Gaya - Departure

After breakfast, you have the option to visit Bodh Gaya to see the Mahabodhi Temple and Bodhi Tree. Later, transfer to Gaya Airport or Railway Station for your return journey.
